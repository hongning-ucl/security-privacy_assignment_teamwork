AdsArmor

Integrated Click-Fraud Defense Demo for PPC Advertising Systems

Three-Layer Defense Architecture

AdsArmor addresses click fraud in pay-per-click advertising systems by combining early-stage filtering, behavioural ML detection, and budget-aware billing control to reduce invalid spend and preserve budget integrity.

System Overview

Incoming Click Stream

Normal and suspicious traffic enters the system as raw click interactions.

Layer 1

Early-stage filtering through rate limiting and challenge-based access control.

Layer 2

Behavioural ML detection using feature extraction, fraud scoring, and anomaly analysis.

Layer 3

Budget-aware billing control through withholding, capping, and mitigation actions.

Billing Outcomes

Final actions preserve budget integrity while supporting service continuity.

Layer 1 Role

Filters obvious high-frequency abuse before deeper behavioural analysis.

Layer 2 Role

Transforms click behaviour into interpretable fraud scores and risk levels.

Layer 3 Role

Converts risk into practical billing actions such as blocking, withholding, and capping.

Key Insights

Layer 2 identifies suspicious behavioural patterns that are not fully removed by Layer 1. By transforming behavioural signals into a continuous fraud score, it improves interpretability and supports downstream billing protection.

Trade-off

Higher sensitivity improves fraud protection but may affect a small amount of high-frequency normal traffic.
